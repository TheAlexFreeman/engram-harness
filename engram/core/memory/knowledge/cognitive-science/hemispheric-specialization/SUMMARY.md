## Key Claims to Remember

- The LH/RH distinction is about **modes of attention**, not function-ownership — both hemispheres process everything, but differently
- The **RH is primary** (the master): it makes first and last contact with reality; it grasps the world as living, unique, relational, embodied
- The **LH is derivative** (the emissary): it re-presents what the RH discloses, in useful but always-abstracted form
- The **corpus callosum is primarily inhibitory** (contested): hemispheres work in parallel, not as one integrated system
- The **evolutionary argument** grounds the asymmetry in bird-level neuroscience, long before human language
- **Schizophrenia** (broad) = LH hyperactivity / RH disconnection; **autism** = reduced social-contextual processing
- TMWT's metaphysics: consciousness is ontologically foundational; reality is processual (Whitehead); time is duration (Bergson); value is intrinsic; the sacred is cognitively real
- The knowledge base has **independently converged** on McGilchrist's core distinction from 10 directions (Vervaeke, Heidegger, Merleau-Ponty, Gibson, Friston, Uexküll, Tononi, Lakoff, Bergson, Damasio)
