# Blending-as-Compression, Structural Coupling, and Subjective Construal
## Relevance to Narrative World-Understanding

A focused deep-dive on three concepts from `cognitive-linguistics-metaphor-blending.md` and their convergence in an account of how narrative functions as a cognitive technology for world-understanding. Related files: `narrative-cognition.md`, `free-energy-autopoiesis-cybernetics.md`, `compression-intelligence-ait.md`.

---

## 1. Blending-as-Compression

### The Mechanism

Fauconnier and Turner's central claim about compression: blending is a cognitive operation that reduces diffuse, extended vital relations into **human-scale inner-space configurations** — representations compact enough for conscious manipulation and imaginative simulation.

The target is always "human scale": a size and temporal range within which we can perceive gestalt structure, apply motor imagination, and run causal simulations without losing track of the whole. The evolutionary assumption is that the cognitive operations refined by selection for navigating the immediate social and physical world are best adapted to human-scale inputs. Blending makes non-human-scale structures cognitively tractable by bringing them to that scale.

The mechanism: **outer-space vital relations** (relations holding across input spaces — analogy between two situations, causal chain extending over years, identity across contexts) are **compressed into inner-space vital relations** (relations holding within the blended space — a single cause, a single agent, a single moment of decision). A long causal chain becomes a single event. Multiple analogical relationships become a single identity. Temporal extension becomes a frozen scene.

Examples:
- "The surgeon is a butcher" compresses a long chain of inferences about professional behavior into a single analogical identity
- The Buddhist Monk riddle (monk walks up the mountain on day 1, walks down on day 2 — will there be a point he occupies at the same time?) compresses two separate journeys into a single blended space where both are present simultaneously, making the answer — which requires imagining them meeting — immediately graspable
- The desktop metaphor compresses a complex computational system (processes, file hierarchies, I/O operations) into a tangible spatial arrangement you can visually manipulate
- A stock market index compresses millions of individual transactions, corporate earnings, geopolitical events, and investor psychology into a single number with direction (up/down) — human-scale enough to feel

### The Information-Theoretic Parallel

The parallel with algorithmic information theory (AIT/Kolmogorov complexity) is suggestive but requires careful qualification.

**The similarity:** Both blending and AIT describe a compression operation that preserves structure while reducing description length. A good blend, like a good program in Kolmogorov terms, captures the regularities — the vital relations — while discarding particulars. Fauconnier's optimality principles (Integration, Topology, Web, Good Reason) are analogous to MDL (Minimum Description Length) criteria: a blend should be as compact as possible while preserving the structure that matters.

**The critical difference:** Kolmogorov complexity is objective — defined relative to a universal prior over programs, measuring the minimum description length in principle, independent of any observer. Blending compression is **observer-relative and niche-specific**: what counts as a good, compact, runnable blend depends entirely on the cognitive system's existing frame structure, background knowledge, and current goals. The same inputs might generate very different blends in different minds — and both might be "optimal" relative to their respective cognitive ecologies.

This difference is actually important: it aligns blending more closely with **Friston's free energy principle** than with Kolmogorov complexity. Friston's organism doesn't compress toward an objective shortest description; it minimizes surprise relative to its *own generative model* — its model of what the world is likely to do. Blending likewise doesn't converge toward objective truth; it converges toward representations that are **coherent and runnable within this system's existing conceptual structure**. The accuracy of narrative compression is ecological validity (fit to the organism's niche), not correspondence accuracy.

This matters: it means the "wisdom" encoded in culturally-transmitted narratives is not objective truth but accumulated ecological fitness — what has worked, for minds like ours, in worlds like this one. It also means that as niches change (as with digital technology, urbanization, globalization), previously well-calibrated narrative templates can become misleading. The mismatch between ancient narrative templates and genuinely novel situations is a permanent source of cognitive distortion.

### Compression Optimality Principles as Narrative Aesthetics

The blending optimality principles read differently when applied to narrative:

- **Integration** (the blend constitutes a tightly runnable scene): narrative integration is plot coherence — the sense that events cohere into a unified action rather than a random sequence
- **Topology** (preserve input structure in the blend): narrative authenticity — the blend should not distort the vital relations of its inputs beyond what serves the integrative purpose
- **Web** (maintain recoverable connections to inputs): narrative legibility — a story should remain decodable back to the situations it compresses. When the web is broken (the blend loses its connections to inputs), we get surrealism or fantasy that cannot be unpacked into lived experience
- **Unpacking**: a well-constructed story can be "unpacked" by a reader — you can trace the inputs from the blend, which is why stories teach: they install a compressed representation that can be elaborated into its source situations when needed
- **Good Reason**: narrative economy — no element should be present without narrative motivation ("Chekhov's gun")

The parallel between blending optimality and narrative aesthetics is not coincidental: narrative *is* blending at the timescale of a life. Literary criticism's vocabulary for good storytelling is, in large part, a vocabulary for blending quality.

## 2. Structural Coupling and Metaphor

### The Core Concept (Maturana/Varela)

Structural coupling is the relationship between an autopoietic system and its medium (environment and other systems). Neither the system nor the medium acts directly on the other; instead, perturbations from the medium trigger **structural changes in the system** that maintain its organization while modifying its specific structure. Over time, a history of recurrent perturbations creates a **structural co-adaptation** between system and medium — neither is unchanged by the encounter.

Critically: the system does not represent the world. It maintains its own organization in response to the world. Cognition is not information-retrieval from an objective database but the history of how a living system has been structurally deformed and stabilized by its encounters with its environment.

### Language as Coordinated Structural Coupling

Maturana developed a specific account of language ("languaging") as a form of living coordination — not symbolic representation but **recursive coordination of coordination**. When two organisms are structurally coupled to each other and to a shared medium, their interactions generate consensual domains: shared behavioral patterns that are not pre-given but emerge from the coupling history.

Language, on this account, arises from recursive structural coupling between organisms. Words don't refer to objects; they trigger structural changes in the listener that coordinate action. Understanding a word is not decoding a symbol but being appropriately deformed by it — having one's neural structure perturbed in a way that coordinates with the speaker's perturbation and with the relevant features of the shared environment.

### Primary Metaphors as Structural Coupling Deposits

This is where Maturana/Varela and Lakoff/Johnson converge most powerfully.

Lakoff/Johnson's **primary metaphors** arise from recurring correlations in embodied experience: warmth and social safety co-occur in early development, so the nervous system develops structural connections between thermal and social processing circuits. The MORE IS UP metaphor arises because quantity and vertical extent co-vary reliably in the physical world (pile things up; the level rises).

In Maturana/Varela's terms, these correlations are the record of structural coupling. The neural circuits that implement primary metaphors are not arbitrary conventions — they are the structural fossils of how a human nervous system, developing in a world with consistent physical regularities (gravity, thermodynamics, force dynamics, social bonding patterns), has been deformed and stabilized by that world over millions of years of evolution and over each individual's developmental history.

**The implication for narrative:** The image schemas and metaphorical mappings that structure narrative are not cognitive inventions but the form that structural coupling takes when it becomes reflexively available to consciousness. When you tell a story that frames a difficulty as a weight you're carrying, you are not merely using a metaphor: you are activating a neural configuration that is structurally continuous with actual embodied experiences of physical burden. The metaphor "works" — produces understanding — because it re-activates circuits that were originally formed by real encounters with force and resistance.

### Languaging, Intersubjectivity, and Shared Coupling History

A crucial extension: structural coupling is not just between an organism and a physical medium but between organisms. The structural coupling between two speakers generates a **shared consensual domain** — a space of coordinated action in which both have been mutually deformed to fit each other's perturbations.

Narrative is the highest form of intersubjective structural coupling: it transmits not just propositions but the structural residue of experience — the patterns of how situations have been encountered, navigated, survived, and understood. When a story is well-told, the listener undergoes a structural change (neural, emotional, attentional) that mirrors, in compressed form, the structural changes that would have been induced by actually living through the narrated events. This is the mechanism of empathy: not inferential perspective-taking but partial structural coupling — having your nervous system shaped by the story into a form that resonates with what it would have been like to be there.

Cultural transmission of narrative, on this account, is the intergenerational transmission of structural coupling wisdom: the accumulated record of how minds like ours have been successfully deformed by worlds like this one.

### The Asymmetry Problem: When Coupling Misleads

The same mechanism that makes narrative understanding reliable also makes it potentially misleading. Structural coupling calibrates the cognitive system to its **historical niche**. When the niche changes rapidly, previously well-calibrated metaphors and narrative templates generate systematic distortions.

Examples:
- Economic metaphors from agrarian/industrial contexts ("the economy is a machine," "labor is a resource") applied to post-industrial, informational economies that have different dynamics
- Evolutionary narrative templates (clear heroes and villains, short causal chains, decisive individual actions) applied to ecological, systemic, or institutional problems where causation is distributed and diffuse
- Social bonding templates from small-group face-to-face societies applied to anonymous mass societies

This is not a failure of the cognitive system but a consequence of its design: structural coupling is conservative, inheriting the structure of past encounters. Genuine novelty — situations that have never been encountered in the relevant niche — is cognitively difficult precisely because no prior coupling has prepared the system for it.

## 3. Subjective Construal and Narrative Viewpoint

### Langacker's Framework

In Ronald Langacker's **Cognitive Grammar**, *construal* covers all the ways a speaker can present the same objective content differently — focusing on different aspects, profiling different elements, adopting different vantage points, construing participants as more or less subjectively or objectively present.

**Subjectivity/objectivity** in construal is about the status of the **construer** (the entity doing the conceptualizing):
- **Objective construal**: the construer (or construer's perspective) is explicitly present in the scene, treated as an object among other objects — "I see the road."
- **Subjective construal**: the construer is offstage, fully absorbed into the vantage point — "The road curves to the left." (The "left" is left from some perspective, but that perspective is not mentioned; it structures the description from behind the scenes.)

At maximum subjectivity, the construer is **maximally backgrounded**: completely present as the organizing perspective but completely absent as an explicit element. The offstage construer is not less real but more pervasive — instead of being one thing in the scene, it *is* the scene's organizing structure.

### Subjectification as Diachronic Tendency

Langacker and (especially) Elizabeth Traugott document **subjectification** as a pervasive tendency in semantic change: expressions that originally described objective, external states come to describe speaker-internal states, epistemic stances, or discourse-structuring functions.

Examples:
- "While" (originally purely temporal: "while you were sleeping") develops epistemic/concessive uses ("While I see your point, I disagree") where the construer's own reasoning process is structured by the connective
- Spatial prepositions develop metaphorical uses tracking the construer's movement through conceptual space ("I'm going through the argument step by step")
- Perceptual verbs (see, feel, smell) develop epistemic readings: "I see what you mean," "I sense something is wrong" — the physical sensory process becomes a metaphor for the construer's internal epistemic process

Sweetser's three-domain model (content → epistemic → speech-act) is the systematic description of subjectification's endpoint: the most fully subjectified uses of modal and connective expressions are those where the relevant entity is the speaker's own reasoning or speech act rather than any element in the objective scene.

### Narrative Construal: Multiple Simultaneous Viewpoints

Narrative is distinctive because it doesn't just adopt one subjective construal — it organizes **multiple simultaneous subjective positions** into a structured whole. The apparatus:

**The narrator's viewpoint:** The organizing subjective position that structures the story — selecting what to include, what to foreground, how to frame events. May be more or less explicit (first-person narrator vs. effaced third-person narrator). In terms of subjectivity, the narrator ranges from maximally objective (Tolstoyan omniscience: God's-eye view of events) to maximally subjective (stream of consciousness: the narrator is fully absorbed into a single perspective, with no offstage position at all).

**Character focalization (Genette):** Whose perspective structures the presentation of events, which may or may not coincide with the narrator. Free indirect discourse — "She was sure he had lied to her" — blends narrator and character perspectives into a single sentence, such that it's impossible to determine whose certainty is being asserted. This is a specifically narrative blend: the organizing frames of narrator-position and character-position both contribute to the blended viewpoint, with emergent structure that is neither pure report nor pure character speech.

**Reader/listener position:** The construer of the whole, who must maintain multiple simultaneous perspectives — narrator's, character's, and their own — while reading. Good narrative writing manages this load carefully, controlling when to align and when to diverge these positions.

### The Blend of Immersion and Distance

The most interesting narrative viewpoint phenomenon is the **dual mode** of story comprehension: readers simultaneously identify with characters (felt sense of what the character experiences) and maintain narrative distance (knowing things characters don't, evaluating characters' choices from outside). This is not a contradiction but a stable double-scope blend:

- **Input 1:** Reader's here-now-I (current perspective, knowledge, affect)
- **Input 2:** Character's there-then-she (story-internal perspective, limited knowledge, embodied affect of the fictional world)
- **Generic space:** An experiencing subject with a point of view
- **Blended space:** The reader who *is* the character — feels what she feels, sees what she sees — while simultaneously *knowing* things the character doesn't and *being* in a reading situation the character isn't in

This blend has emergent structure: the reader can feel genuine dread on behalf of a character approaching danger that the reader, but not the character, knows is present. Neither pure detachment (I'm just watching) nor pure identification (I'm lost in the character's subjectivity) — but a stable third thing that is the specific cognitive achievement of narrative comprehension.

Sweetser's work on viewpoint in gesture supports this: speakers telling stories spontaneously shift between narrator and character positions, marked by changes in body orientation, gesture space, and gaze direction. The blend is not just conceptual but enacted in the full semiotic performance of storytelling.

## 4. Synthesis: Narrative as Triangulated World-Understanding

The three mechanisms together constitute what might be called **narrative triangulation** — the cognitive achievement by which narrative understanding is both tractable and accurate (to the extent that it is):

**Compression (blending)** makes the world cognitively tractable. Causal-temporal structures that would be impossibly large to hold in working memory are compressed into human-scale blends that can be imaginatively simulated, reasoned about, and remembered as episodes. The compression is lossy, but it is lossy in a principled way: what is preserved are the vital relations — especially the agentive-causal, force-dynamic structure — that matter for a social agent navigating a world of other social agents.

**Structural coupling (inherited metaphorical structure)** makes the compression accurate. The image schemas and force-dynamic templates that structure narrative are not arbitrary conventions but are calibrated by long histories of structural coupling between minds like ours and worlds like this one. The narrative understanding of a situation is accurate insofar as the situation falls within the organism's evolutionary-cultural niche — insofar as the accumulated coupling history has deposited structures appropriate to this situation. It fails when situations are genuinely novel relative to the niche.

**Subjective construal (multiple simultaneous viewpoints)** makes the understanding actionable. Narrative knowledge is not merely propositional — it includes the felt sense of what it is like to be in a situation, approached from multiple angles simultaneously. The blended viewpoint of narrative comprehension (immersed-and-distanced) allows the reader to run the narrative as a simulation of what it would be like to face the situation from the inside while retaining the evaluative distance to draw conclusions that the character cannot draw. This is what makes narrative the preeminent vehicle for moral and practical wisdom: it produces knowledge that is neither purely abstract (propositional rule) nor purely experiential (lived encounter) but a blend of both.

### The Dark Side

The same three mechanisms that make narrative a powerful tool for world-understanding make it the primary tool for world-misrepresentation:

- Compression loses structure: the facts that don't fit the narrative template are discarded, producing systematic blind spots
- Structural coupling is historically calibrated: ancient templates applied to novel situations generate confident misunderstandings
- The immersive blend disables critical distance: at maximum narrative absorption, the reader adopts the story's values and sympathies without the evaluative distance needed to notice that they are being channeled

Political propaganda and advertising work precisely through narrative: they exploit all three mechanisms simultaneously. The power and the danger are inseparable — which is one reason narrative literacy (the capacity to recognize and critically evaluate the structural features of narratives rather than being absorbed by their content) is among the more important cognitive competencies.
