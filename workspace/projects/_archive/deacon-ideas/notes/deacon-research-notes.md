**Terrence Deacon Core Concepts (from research):**

**Key Books:**
- *The Symbolic Species* (1997): Coevolution of language and brain. Humans as the symbolic species. Symbolic reference is combinatorial, learned via relationships between signs (not direct object reference). Builds on Peirce: icons (similarity), indexes (causal/contiguous), symbols (arbitrary, system-dependent, conventional). Symbolic threshold crossed in human evolution, leading to brain changes (larger prefrontal, changes in vocal tract, etc.). Co-evolution: language shaped brain, brain shaped language.

**Incomplete Nature** (2011): How mind emerged from matter. Addresses the "incomplete" science that leaves out meaning, purpose, consciousness. Introduces:
- **Absential phenomena**: Causes that are absent yet efficacious (e.g., a hole in a wheel constrains its function; purpose is "about" something not present). "Absence that makes a difference."
- **Ententional**: Umbrella for intentionality, teleology, function, meaning, purpose — all "incomplete" in referring to something extrinsic.
- **Constraints**: Central. What is *not* possible (absent possibilities) that shape dynamics. Constraints propagate causality in emergent ways. Contrasts with efficient/material causes.
- **Orthograde vs Contragrade**: Spontaneous tendencies (orthograde = toward equilibrium or self-organization); contragrade work opposes them.
- **Three dynamics** (nested):
  - **Homeodynamics**: Thermodynamic, toward equilibrium, dissipation of gradients (2nd law). Entropy increase.
  - **Morphodynamics**: Self-organizing forms from coupled homeodynamics (e.g., Bénard cells, snowflakes, whirlpools). Generates constraints spontaneously. Amplifies order.
  - **Teleodynamics**: Emerges from coupled morphodynamics that mutually constrain each other to self-maintain, reproduce, adapt. Produces "ententional" properties — life, mind, semiosis. Self-reconstituting systems (autogens as minimal example). Teleodynamics is the basis for function, purpose, information as constraint.

**Autogen**: Hypothetical minimal teleodynamic unit — autocatalytic + self-assembling molecules that self-repair/reproduce via reciprocal constraints.

**Biosemiotics/Peirce**: Strong integration. Semiosis at heart of life. Symbolic species extends biosemiotics to human uniqueness. Language as prime mover in evolution.

**Implications**:
